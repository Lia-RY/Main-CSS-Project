This is how Coffee Zero works

Coffee Zero contains chromium which contributes to the normal turnover of macronutrients. Simply explained, this means that chromium helps the body burn carbohydrates, proteins and fats.

The feeling of hunger and sweet tooth can be caused by large fluctuations in blood sugar. Coffee Zero contains chromium which helps to maintain a normal blood sugar level.

With less sweet tooth, it can be easier to make healthy food choices and to avoid snacking between meals.

Coffee Zero helps you maintain the good habits.

At the same time, we remind you that this is not a conclusion for everyone. Many customers say, for example, that their sweet tooth is noticeably reduced after just a few days or weeks of Coffee Zero. Others have to wait longer before they experience the same. This is linked to the individual's prerequisites in terms of diet, metabolism, everyday habits and more.
