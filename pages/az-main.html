One of the most celebrated and traditional main courses is "Plov" or "Pilaf."

Plov is a delicious rice dish cooked in a flavorful broth, typically featuring tender meat (chicken, lamb, beef, or fish) and vegetables. The dish is often enriched with aromatic spices, giving it a unique and tantalizing taste.

Category: main course

Calories: 700kcal

Time: 30 minutes
