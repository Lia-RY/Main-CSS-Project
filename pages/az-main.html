Shah Plov

Cook Time: 1 hour 20 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 Lavash (thin flatbread)
  • 500 grams of meat
  • 210 g of steamed rice
  • 120 g onions
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 150 g carrot
  • 70 g seedless raisins
  • 10 g of barberry
  • 5 g sweet smoked paprika
  • 3 g ground red pepper
  • 2 g of saffron
  • 120 g butter
  • vegetable oil, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Pour 250 ml of water into a saucepan, add rice, add 30 g of butter and salt. After boiling, close the lid, cook for 12 minutes on low heat, and steam for another 10 minutes, covering the pot with a towel
  2. In a frying pan, pour 2-3 tablespoons of sunflower oil, throw in diced meat into warmed butter. Usually, shah-pilaf in pita bread is cooked with lamb or veal. I hold the opinion that the meat that is most popular in your latitudes is appropriate. Nothing terrible will happen to the recipe, if in central Russia they cook pilaf with pork. Learn as tasty, rest assured! Finely chop the onion and garlic, add to the meat, fry a few minutes all together. To the fried meat, add raisins soaked in tea, barberries, imeretinsky saffron and ground red pepper, salt.
  3. Put the meat out of the pan on a plate. In the same pan, put the carrot sliced ​​into cubes, fry until soft several minutes, sprinkle with salt and sweet paprika.
  4. Melt the remaining butter in a frying pan. Cut thin pita bread in wide strips.
  5. Lubricate them with a thin layer of melted butter and place in a cast-iron pan with a fan.
  6. We divide the finished rice in half, put one part on the bottom of the pan, pour the butter.
  7. Then spread passerovannye carrots, distribute evenly.Add meat with spices, also leveled.Put the rest of the rice on the meat, pour the butter.
  8. Wrap the edges of the pita overlap, pour with oil. Cover the pot with a lid.Cooking Shah-Plov in pita bread 50 minutes-1 hour in an oven heated to 170 degrees
  9. mmediately turn the finished Shah-plov onto a plate, serve it hot.
