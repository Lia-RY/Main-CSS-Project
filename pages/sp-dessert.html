Today, we'll discover a beloved Spanish dessert, "Crema Catalana"

                Crema Catalana is a traditional Spanish custard dessert with a caramelized sugar topping. 
                It's similar to crème brûlée and is a delight for your taste buds.

Category: dessert

Calories: 300kcal

Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  1. 4 egg yolks
  2. 500 ml milk
  3. 1 cinnamon stick
  4. 1 strip of lemon peel
  5. 100 g granulated sugar
  6. 30 g cornstarch
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Extra granulated sugar for caramelizing

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk with the cinnamon stick and lemon peel until it starts to simmer. Remove from heat and let it infuse for 10-15 minutes. Then, remove the cinnamon stick and lemon peel.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the egg yolks with the granulated sugar until creamy and pale yellow.
  3. Add cornstarch to the egg yolk mixture and mix until smooth and well combined.
  4. Gradually pour the infused milk into the egg yolk mixture while continuously whisking to avoid curdling.
  5. Transfer the mixture back to the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens to a custard consistency. This should take about 5-10 minutes.
  6. Remove the custard from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  7. Pour the custard into individual serving dishes or ramekins and let them cool to room temperature. Then, refrigerate them for at least 2 hours to set.
  8. When ready to serve, sprinkle a thin layer of granulated sugar over each custard. Caramelize the sugar using a kitchen torch or by placing the dishes under a preheated broiler until the sugar turns golden and crispy.
  9. Allow the caramelized sugar to cool and harden before serving.
  10. Enjoy your delicious Crema Catalana!
