Today, we'll explore a classic Romanian dish, "Mămăligă with Cheese and Sour Cream."

                Mămăligă is a traditional Romanian dish made from coarse cornmeal, water, and salt.
                It has a porridge-like consistency and is a staple in Romanian households.

Category

Calories

Time

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup yellow cornmeal (coarse cornmeal)
  2. 4 cups water
  3. 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  4. 1 cup grated Romanian brânză (a type of sheep's or cow's milk cheese) or substitute with feta cheese or other crumbly cheese
  5. Sour cream, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the water to a boil over high heat.
  2. Once the water is boiling, gradually pour in the cornmeal while stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Lower the heat to medium-low.
  3. Add the salt and continue stirring the mixture frequently to prevent sticking or burning. Cook for about 20-25 minutes or until the polenta reaches a thick and creamy consistency. The cooking time may vary based on the type of cornmeal used.
  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the polenta rest for a few minutes.
  5. While the polenta is still warm, stir in the grated brânză or your preferred cheese until well combined. The cheese will melt slightly into the polenta, adding extra flavor.
  6. Serve the polenta warm in individual bowls or on a serving platter. Top each portion with a generous dollop of sour cream.
  7. Optionally, you can garnish with some extra grated cheese or chopped fresh herbs for added taste and presentation.
