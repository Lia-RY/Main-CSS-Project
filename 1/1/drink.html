Atlama

Super refreshing and healthy drink

Ingredients:

  • 8 cup yogurt
  • 10 cup filtered still water
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 smal diced cucumber
  • corriander, purple basil

Instructions:

  1. mix yogurt and water together until its smooth.
  2. cut greens and add together with cucumber
  3. add salt and some ice cubes and serve
