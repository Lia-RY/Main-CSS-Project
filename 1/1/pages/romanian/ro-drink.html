Discover the spirit of Romania with "Tuică" - the traditional Romanian alcoholic beverage.

                Tuică is a clear and strong alcoholic drink made through the distillation of fermented fruits, most commonly plums.
                It is considered an essential part of Romanian culture and traditions.

Category: drink

Calories: 70kcal

Time: 2 weeks

Ingredients:

  1. 8-10 kilograms pears
  2. 8-10 kilograms apples
  3. Apricots apricots

Instructions

  1. Crush the ripe plums or extract the juice to create the fruit mash.
  2. Place the crushed plums or plum juice in a fermentation container.
  3. Allow the plums to ferment naturally for about 1-2 weeks.
  4. After fermentation, distill the fermented plum mash using a traditional copper still or distillation equipment to separate alcohol from water and impurities.
  5. Bottle the freshly distilled Tuică for aging or immediate consumption.
  6. If desired, age the Tuică in oak barrels for several months to enhance its flavor.
  7. Tuică is a strong alcoholic beverage, so drink it responsibly and in moderation.
