Today, we'll discover a classic Romanian soup, "Ciorbă de Burtă."

                Ciorbă de Burtă is a hearty and flavorful soup made from beef tripe, vegetables, and sour cream.
                It's a popular dish in Romania, known for its rich taste and comforting qualities.

Category: main

Calories: 245kcal

Time: 2 hours

Ingredients:

  1. 500 grams beef tripe
  2. 2 liters water of beef broth
  3. 1-2 medium-sized vegetables (carrots, onions, bell peppers, celery)
  4. 2 tablespoons tomato paste or diced tomatoes
  5. 2-3 cloves garlic
  6. 1/2 cup sour cream
  7. 2 tablespoonsvinegar
  8. 2-3 leavesbay leaves
  9. to tastesalt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut the beef tripe into small pieces.
  2. Boil the beef tripe in water or beef broth until tender (approximately 1-1.5 hours).
  3. Chop the vegetables (carrots, onions, bell peppers, celery) and mince the garlic.
  4. Sauté the vegetables until tender and slightly caramelized.
  5. Add the sautéed vegetables and tomatoes (tomato paste or diced tomatoes) to the pot with the cooked tripe.
  6. Stir in sour cream, vinegar, or lemon juice for a tangy flavor.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Let the soup simmer for 15-20 minutes to meld the flavors.
  9. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley and dill.
  10. Serve hot in bowls and enjoy your comforting Ciorbă de Burtă!
