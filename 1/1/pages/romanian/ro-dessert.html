Today, we'll discover a beloved Romanian dessert, "Papanași."

                Papanași are traditional Romanian fried doughnuts, often described as a mix between a doughnut and a dumpling.
                They are typically made with cottage cheese and are known for their fluffy texture and indulgent taste.

Category: dessert

Calories: 900kcal

Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  1. 250 g cottage cheese (preferably dry and drained of excess liquid)
  2. 150 g all-purpose flour
  3. 1 large egg
  4. 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  5. 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  6. 1lemon zest
  7. oil for frying
  8. Sour cream, fruit jam for serving

Instructions

  1. Mix together cottage cheese, egg, sugar, and optional lemon zest.
  2. Add flour and baking powder to the mixture. Mix until you have a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into doughnut-like rounds.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan.
  5. Fry the doughnuts until golden brown and crispy on both sides (about 2-3 minutes per side).
  6. Fry the doughnuts until golden brown and crispy on both sides (about 2-3 minutes per side).
  7. Serve warm and top with sour cream and fruit jam or dust with powdered sugar or honey.
