Salad Mangal

Roasted Vegetables from Azerbaijan

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggplants
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 green pepper

Instructions:

  1. Grill all the vegetables in the oven or bbq. pill and set aside to drain its water.
  2. wait for cooling. then cut in a small bites
  3. Mix all the ingridients together, add salt and pepper ,then add olive oil and serve
